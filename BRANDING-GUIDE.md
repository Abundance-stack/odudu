# UISS Branding Guide

## Official School Name

**Full Name:** UISS International Secondary School

**Short Name:** UISS

## Usage Guidelines

### When to Use FULL NAME

Use "UISS International Secondary School" in:

1. **Page Titles & Meta Tags**
   - `<title>UISS International Secondary School - Excellence and Creativity</title>`
   - Meta descriptions

2. **Hero Sections / Main Headers**
   - Homepage hero: "UISS International Secondary School"
   - About page introduction

3. **Official Communications**
   - Welcome messages
   - Principal's message
   - Formal letters

4. **Footer**
   - Footer heading: "UISS International Secondary School"
   - Copyright: "© 2026 UISS International Secondary School"

5. **First Mention in Content**
   - First paragraph of any page
   - History section
   - About us content

6. **External Links**
   - WhatsApp: "Hello UISS International Secondary School"
   - Social media references

### When to Use SHORT NAME

Use "UISS" in:

1. **Navigation Logo**
   - `<span class="logo-text">UISS</span>`

2. **Subsequent Mentions**
   - After first mention in content
   - Casual references
   - Testimonials

3. **Headings**
   - "Why Choose UISS?"
   - "UISS transformed my son's academic journey"

4. **Buttons & CTAs**
   - "Join UISS"
   - "Apply to UISS"

5. **Internal References**
   - "At UISS, we believe..."
   - "UISS stands as a beacon..."

## Brand Consistency

### DO:
✅ Use full name in formal contexts
✅ Use full name in first mention
✅ Use short name for brevity after introduction
✅ Be consistent within each page

### DON'T:
❌ Mix "UNIUYO ISS" with "UISS"
❌ Use only initials without context
❌ Change branding mid-sentence
❌ Use different variations randomly

## Examples

### Good Usage:

**Formal Introduction:**
> "Welcome to UISS International Secondary School, where we are committed to excellence in education. At UISS, we believe that every student can achieve greatness."

**Casual Reference:**
> "UISS transformed my son's academic journey. The teachers are dedicated and the environment is conducive for learning."

**Call to Action:**
> "Join UISS International Secondary School today and start your journey to excellence!"

### Bad Usage:

❌ "Welcome to UISS. At UISS International Secondary School, we..."
❌ "UNIUYO ISS is the best school..."
❌ "Join UISS Int'l Sec. School..."
