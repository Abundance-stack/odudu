Admission Process

1

Obtain Application Form

Purchase the admission form from the school office or download it from our website. Form costs ‚Ç¶5,000 and includes entrance examination fee.

2

Complete Application

Fill out the application form completely and accurately. Attach all required documents including birth certificate, previous school results, and passport photographs.

3

Submit Application

Submit completed form with all documents to the school office or via email. You will receive an acknowledgment and entrance exam schedule.

4

Take Entrance Examination

Attend the scheduled entrance examination. Exams cover Mathematics, English Language, and General Knowledge. Bring writing materials and exam slip.

5

Receive Admission Letter

Successful candidates will receive admission letters within 2 weeks. Check results online or collect from school office.

6

Pay School Fees & Resume

Accept admission by paying school fees before the deadline. Attend orientation and resume classes on the specified date.

Admission Requirements

For JSS1 Admission:

  • Completed application form
  • Birth certificate or age declaration
  • Primary school leaving certificate
  • Common Entrance result (if available)
  • 4 recent passport photographs
  • Previous school report card
  • Medical fitness certificate
  • Parent/Guardian identification

For SS1 Admission:

  • Completed application form
  • Birth certificate or age declaration
  • JSS3 certificate
  • BECE result (minimum of 5 passes)
  • 4 recent passport photographs
  • Previous school testimonial
  • Medical fitness certificate
  • Parent/Guardian identification

Age Requirements:

  • JSS1: 10-12 years old
  • SS1: 14-16 years old
  • Transfer students: Age appropriate for class level

Entrance Examination

Exam Details:

Subjects Tested:

  • Mathematics (40 questions - 45 minutes)
  • English Language (40 questions - 45 minutes)
  • General Knowledge (20 questions - 30 minutes)

Exam Format: Multiple choice questions

Pass Mark: 50% aggregate

Exam Dates: Every last Saturday of the month

Exam Time: 9:00 AM prompt

Venue: School Examination Hall

What to Bring:

  • Examination slip (provided after registration)
  • Writing materials (pen, pencil, eraser, ruler)
  • Calculator (for Mathematics only)
  • Water bottle

Frequently Asked Questions

When does admission open? +

Admission opens in January for the new academic session starting in September. However, we accept applications throughout the year for available spaces.

Can I transfer my child from another school? +

Yes, we accept transfer students. You'll need to provide a transfer certificate, previous school report cards, and your child must pass our entrance examination.

Do you offer scholarships? +

Yes, we offer merit-based scholarships to exceptional students. Scholarships cover 25-50% of tuition fees and are awarded based on entrance exam performance and academic excellence.

Is boarding facility available? +

Currently, we operate as a day school only. However, we have plans to introduce boarding facilities in the near future.

What is the student-teacher ratio? +

We maintain a maximum ratio of 25:1 to ensure personalized attention and effective learning for every student.

Do you provide transportation? +

Yes, we have school buses covering major routes in Uyo. Transportation fee is ‚Ç¶30,000 per term and varies by location.

What is your WAEC success rate? +

We consistently achieve 95-98% pass rate in WAEC with majority of students scoring 5 credits and above including English and Mathematics.

Can I visit the school before applying? +

Absolutely! We encourage parents to visit. Contact us to schedule a tour. School tours are available Monday-Friday, 10 AM - 2 PM.
