About UISS

Our History

UNIUYO International Secondary School was established in with a vision to provide world-class secondary education in Akwa Ibom State. Founded by a group of dedicated educators and community leaders, our school has grown from a modest beginning of 45 students to become one of the most respected secondary institutions in the region.

Over the years, we have maintained our commitment to academic excellence, producing outstanding WAEC and NECO results consistently. Our alumni have gone on to study in prestigious universities both in Nigeria and abroad, making significant contributions to society in various fields.

Today, U International Secondary School stands as a beacon of quality education, combining traditional values with modern teaching methodologies to prepare students for the challenges of the 21st century.
